Now

Now

This page answers a simple question: what am I working on right now?

It’s inspired by the /now page movement — a way to share what’s currently occupying my attention, rather than what’s polished and permanent.

Work

Building software at Smartly.io, where I work on backend systems and product engineering. Currently focused on scaling systems and improving how we think about data pipelines.

Reading

Splitting my attention across several books as always:

  • At the desk: Currently working through philosophy and technical writing
  • By the bed: A novel that’s been following me home for weeks
  • In the bag: Essays and short pieces for the commute

The exact titles rotate, but the three-books-in-parallel approach keeps all versions of myself engaged.

Photography

Shooting mostly on Berlin streets — looking for light, quiet moments, and the way cities reveal themselves to patient eyes. The usual haunts: Kreuzberg, Wedding, Friedrichshain. Some experiments with different approaches to composition.

Projects

Working on a few small corners of the internet that don’t quite fit anywhere else. Not all of them are ready for public attention, but they’re keeping me curious.

Photography Platforms

You can see more recent work on:

  • Instagram — curated street / travel photography
  • Instagram — personal thoughts and tangential images
  • Flickr — archive of older work

Writing

Drafting thoughts on engineering, books, photography, and the spaces where these things intersect. Not everything makes it to publication, but the writing habit persists.
